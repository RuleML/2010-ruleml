Sponsorship Information

We are pleased to offer the opportunity to be a sponsor of the 4th International Web Rule Symposium (RuleML-2010).

RuleML-2010 is the primary industry-oriented event of the Web Logic community that again (following the previous three editions) joins the established, practically-oriented Forum of the Business Rules community to help cross-fertilization of ideas between Web and Business Logic technologies. It is devoted to practical distributed rule technologies and rule-based applications which need language standards for rules operating in the context of, amongst other things, the Semantic Web, Intelligent Multi-Agent Systems, Event-Driven Architectures and Service-Oriented Computing Applications.

RuleML-2010 provides an excellent opportunity for your organization to be associated with an innovation driven technology market, characterized by strong market growth, visibility and commercial opportunities.
